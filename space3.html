Best Space Organization

17 June 2022

7. JAXA Full Form: Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency Founded: 1 October 2003 Country: Japan Ranked: 7th About: The Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency is the Japanese national aerospace and space agency. Through the merger of three previously independent organizations, JAXA was formed on 1 October 2003.

6. CNSA Full Form: China National Space Administration Founded: 22 April 1993 Country: China Ranked: 6th About: China National Space Administration is the national space agency of China responsible for the national space program and for planning and development of space activities.

5. SpaceX Full Form: Space Exploration Founded: 6 May 2002 Country: United States of America Ranked: 5th About: Space Exploration Technologies Corp. is an American aerospace manufacturer and space transportation services company headquartered in Hawthorne, California. It was founded in 2002 by Elon Musk with the goal of reducing space transportation costs to enable the colonization of Mars.

4. ISRO Full Form: Indian Space Research Organization Founded: 15 August 1969 Country: India Ranked: 4th About: The Indian Space Research Organization or is the national space agency of the Republic of India, headquartered in Bengaluru. It operates under Department of Space which is directly overseen by the prime minister of India while chairman of ISRO acts as executive of DOS as well.

3. ESA Full Form: European Space Agency Founded: 30 May 1975 Country: Europe Ranked: 3rd About: The European Space Agency is an intergovernmental organization of 22 member states dedicated to the exploration of space. Established in 1975 and headquartered in Paris, ESA has a worldwide staff of about 2,200 in 2018 and an annual budget of about €6.68 billion in 2020

2. Roscosmos Full Form: Roscosmos State Corporation for Space Activities Founded: 25 February 1992 Country: Russia Ranked: 2nd About: The Roscosmos State Corporation for Space Activities, commonly known as Roscosmos, is a state corporation of the Russian Federation responsible for space flights, cosmonautics programs, and aerospace research.

1. NASA Full Form: National Aeronautics and Space Administration Founded: 1 October 1958 Country: United States of America Ranked: 1st About: The National Aeronautics and Space Administration is an independent agency of the U.S. federal government responsible for the civilian space program, as well as aeronautics and space research. NASA was established in 1958, succeeding the National Advisory Committee for Aeronautics.
